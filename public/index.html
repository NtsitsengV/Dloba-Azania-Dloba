About Us

At Dloba Azania, we are dedicated to unlocking the full potential of every young person, regardless of their background. Our mission is to provide underprivileged youth with access to a rich variety of activities, including sports, arts, and cultural programs, with a special focus on cricket. Through our comprehensive and supportive approach, we aim to foster personal growth, enhance teamwork, and encourage creative expression, helping each individual to thrive and achieve their best.

Services

Sports

Empower children and teenagers with leagues, clinics, and training sessions that promote fitness, teamwork, and community spirit. Our well-equipped and maintained facilities provide a safe environment for youth to thrive.

Culture

Celebrating cultural diversity through vibrant festivals, parades, and exhibitions. Our events provide opportunities to experience and learn about different traditions, fostering community connections through engaging activities, classes, and gatherings.

Art

Art Classes and Workshops: Inspiring youth to explore their creativity through painting, drawing, sculpture, and more. Exhibitions and Shows: Showcasing local talent by organizing art exhibitions that celebrate creativity and foster community engagement.

Games

Celebrating and preserving indigenous traditions through engaging and educational games like diketo, morabaraba, ncuva, juskei, and sack races. Our games foster community bonds and keep cultural heritage alive through the joy of play.
